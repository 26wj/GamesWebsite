Stronghold Crusader HD Maps

Installation Instructions:

  1. Go to your PC's C: or D: drive.
  2. Navigate to the Steam folder.
  3. Then go to steamapps > common directory.
  4. Look for the Stronghold Crusader HD folder.
  5. Click on the maps folder inside the Stronghold Crusader HD folder.
  6. Finally, paste the downloaded maps in this folder.
